After having worked on websites and web applications for about eight years, it occurred to me that I've never had my own website. Now I've decided to start a blog. I have all kinds of ideas for it. These for instance:

  • Content:
    • Observations: everyday, technical, both or other
    • Plans and ideas I have
    • Silly rhymes or other jokes
    • Technical posts, e.g. about this blog
    • Bilingual, in Dutch and English
    • I might even make up a story one day
  • Technical:
    • Link the blog to a github repository
      • Automatically commit when saving and publishing posts
      • Add conversations with language models to posts where I used them
    • Backups
    • I might even do something about the look of the posts and website some day

I intend to post at least once every month. I write for me primarily, but such an objective helps me keep this up.
