I am Koen, and this is my blog. Here I post a piece of writing at least once a month. It can be anything, both in form and content. Whatever flows from my (nowadays usually just metaphorical) pen when I put it to (similarly metaphorical) paper. I do identify as a software developer, among other things, so there is a substantial risk that the subject matter will occasionally be technical in nature.
